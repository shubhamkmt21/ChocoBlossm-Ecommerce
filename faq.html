Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do you ship all over India?

Yes, we ship to over 25,000 pincodes across India. This includes all major metro cities and most tier-2/3 cities.

2. Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?

Yes, Cash on Delivery is available for orders below ₹5,000. For orders above this amount, we request prepaid payment for security reasons.

3. Will the chocolate melt during transit?

We use specialized heat-resistant packaging with thermal insulation and ice gels to ensure your chocolates withstand the Indian climate. In extreme summer, we may delay shipment slightly to avoid heatwaves.

4. Can I get a GST Invoice?

Absolutely. If you need a GST invoice for corporate gifting, please enter your GSTIN number in the 'Notes' section during checkout or email us immediately after placing the order.

5. Do you offer customization for weddings or corporate events?

Yes, we specialize in bulk orders and custom packaging! Please visit our 'Contact Us' page or WhatsApp us at +91 98765 43210 for a catalog.

6. How should I store the chocolates?

Store them in a cool, dry place (ideally 18°C - 20°C). Do not freeze them directly as it changes the texture. If refrigerating, keep them in an airtight container to avoid condensation.
