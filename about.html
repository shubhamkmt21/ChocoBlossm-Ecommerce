The Choco Blossom Story

"Where passion meets premium cocoa"

Crafted with Love in Mumbai

Founded in 2010, Choco Blossom India began with a simple mission: to bring world-class artisanal Belgian-style chocolates to Indian connoisseurs. What started as a small home kitchen project has now blossomed into a beloved brand known for its impeccable quality and innovative flavors.

We source our cocoa beans from sustainable farms in Ghana and Madagascar, ensuring that every bite you take is ethical, rich, and truly authentic.

100% Handcrafted

Every piece is molded and decorated by hand by our master chocolatiers.

Ethically Sourced

We support fair trade practices and sustainable farming.
